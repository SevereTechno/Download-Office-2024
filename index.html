More About this app

Microsoft Office is a family of client software, server software, and services developed by Microsoft. Initially launched in 1988, Office consists of Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Over time, Office applications have evolved with shared features like a common spell checker, Object Linking and Embedding data integration, and Visual Basic for Applications scripting language. Office is also marketed as a development platform for line-of-business software under the Office Business Applications brand. Office is available in various versions, with the desktop version being the most widely used. Microsoft also maintains mobile apps for Android and iOS, and Office on the web is a version that runs within a web browser. Since 2013, Microsoft has promoted Office 365 as the primary means of obtaining Microsoft Office, offering a subscription business model with feature updates for the lifetime of the subscription. Revenue from Office 365 overtook conventional license sales in 2017. Microsoft rebranded most standard Office 365 editions as "Microsoft 365" to reflect their inclusion of features and services beyond the core Microsoft Office suite. In October 2022, Microsoft announced phasing out the Microsoft Office brand in favor of "Microsoft 365" by January 2023, but reversed this stance with the release of an Office 2024 preview build in November 2023.
